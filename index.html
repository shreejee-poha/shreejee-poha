Welcome to Shreejee Poha Udyog

📍 Balaghat, Madhya Pradesh



We are a leading manufacturer of raw rice and poha. With a legacy of quality spanning several decades, we take pride in offering the finest grains to our customers. Our dedication to excellence has led to the creation of our own renowned brands in the market.

At Shreejee Poha Udyog, we don't just stop at rice and poha; we are also your trusted source for other rice-related products, including broken rice, rice husk, rice bran, and poha churi. We specialize in all types of paddy, ensuring that you have a one-stop solution for all your rice requirements.

Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction sets us apart. We are dedicated to delivering products that meet the highest industry standards, and we take pride in serving our valued customers with integrity and dedication.

Explore our website to learn more about our offerings, our commitment to quality, and how to get in touch with us. We look forward to serving your needs with excellence.
